Types of Equipment Financing

At SD Loans and Leasing, we offer various types of equipment financing to ensure you have the financial support you need:

  • Operating Leases: Provides short-term financing for equipment use without ownership.
  • Finance Leases: Offers long-term financing with an option to purchase the equipment at the end of the lease term.
  • Equipment Loans: Provides funds to purchase business equipment and machinery.
  • Sale and Leaseback: Allows you to sell your existing equipment and lease it back for continued use.
